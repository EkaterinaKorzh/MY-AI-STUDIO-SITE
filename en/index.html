Questions

Everything you need to know before your first photoshoot.

Will the photos look like me?
Yes. The portraits are created based on your photos to preserve your appearance, facial expressions, and key features.

We use your images as a foundation and build the photoshoot so that the final result looks natural and recognizable.
What photos should I upload?
3–5 regular photos taken on a phone in good lighting are enough.

The best photos are high-quality pictures where you like how you look and appear natural. Such photos help the system more accurately capture your appearance, facial expressions, hairstyle, and overall look.

To make the portraits look as close to you as possible, it is recommended that your photos include:

• a clearly visible face with good lighting
• different angles — with and without a smile
• the hairstyle you want to see in the final portraits
• visible hair length.

For example, if you want portraits with your hair down, it is best to upload photos where your hair is styled that way.

For women, photos with natural loose hair or light styling usually work best — this helps more accurately capture the shape, volume, and length of the hair.

The more natural and well-chosen your original photos are, the more realistic, recognizable, and beautiful your final portraits will be.
Do I need to book a session or communicate with a photographer?
No.

The entire photoshoot happens directly inside the Telegram bot.

You simply choose a style, select a package, and upload your photos — the bot guides you through every step.
How long does it take to create the photoshoot?
The photos are ready within 24 hours, often faster if no additional manual refinement is required.
Can I continue the photoshoot if I like the results?
Yes.

If you like the style and look, you can add more photos within the same photoshoot.

The bot will preserve the style, background, and overall atmosphere so the new images look like a natural continuation.

Yes.
